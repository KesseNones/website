Summary

I am Jesse and this is my website. Take a look around and enjoy what lives here. This site is the standard portfolio. It exists to showcase: my projects, who I am, what I want to do, and the other usual portfolio stuff. My intent is a website that's largely minimal in design, making it look good and easy to load simultaneously. Most sites are too flashy, too fancy. We need sites that remind us of the ancient days of simple sites made almost totally of HTML. That's what I strive for here, with some exceptions for demos.

Thanks and have fun!
