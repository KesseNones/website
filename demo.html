3D Spaceship Demo


Controls (click me to toggle!):
  • WSAD for camera movement.
  • Arrow Keys for camera look controls.
  • K to lock with spaceship upward/downward movement.
  • L to unlock with spaceship upward/downward movement.
  • C to move directly down.
  • Space to move directly up.
  • Q to rotate camera counter-clockwise.
  • E to rotate camera clockwise.
  • Refresh to reset the world.
